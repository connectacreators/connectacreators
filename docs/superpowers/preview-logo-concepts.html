Brand exploration

10 logo concepts, one brand.

Each concept uses only the locked palette (Ink, Bone, Aqua, Honey) and the locked fonts (EB Garamond, Figtree). They range from pure wordmark to symbol+mark — pick a direction and you can take it from there.

01
Connecta
Pure wordmark · current usage

The Italic A

EB Garamond. The final "a" italicized in honey, slightly enlarged. Refined, editorial, the most "magazine masthead" of the set. Works at any size, scales gracefully to favicon.

— what's already on the site.
02
connect · a
Mixed type · masthead

The Interpunct

Figtree sans for "connect" + honey interpunct + EB Garamond italic "a". A typographic punctuation gag — the "a" is the conjunction. Feels like a literary journal column header.

— pairs well with thin horizontal rules.
03
C · a
Monogram · favicon-ready

C·a

Just the two letters with a centered honey dot. Compact and stamp-able — perfect for app icon, social avatar, wax-seal moments. Works on dark or light backgrounds.

— think SoHo gallery business card.
04
Connecta
Symbol + wordmark · the connection

Linked Dots

Two filled circles — honey (creator) and aqua (client/brand) — joined by a hand-drawn arc. Literal and unmissable: this is what Connecta does. Works as standalone symbol when there's no room for the wordmark.

— the most "what does the product do" mark.
05
Connecta
Strategic mark · the brain

Atlas Knot

One continuous aqua line that crosses itself in the middle — infinity-knot meets node graph. The honey dot at the cross is the brand's moment of "connection." Most Jarvis / Super-Canvas-coded.

— this one would animate beautifully on landing.
06
Connecta
Symbol + wordmark · creator vision

The All-Seeing Eye

A horizontal eye outline with a honey sparkle inside the pupil. "We see what's about to go viral before you do." Slightly mystical — leans into the Jarvis-as-oracle metaphor.

— very memorable but a stronger commitment.
07
Connecta
Symbol + wordmark · creator camera

The Aperture

Camera-aperture shape with alternating bone/aqua/honey blades. Literally a C through the negative space. Says "creator tool" without saying the word.

— very brand-system-friendly. Each blade can rotate as motion.
08
C
Connecta
Hand-drawn mark · the scribble

The Lasso C

An italic serif C inside a hand-drawn honey scribble lasso. The most wisprflow-direct — same energy as the "most loved" badge on the pricing card. Personality without losing seriousness.

— most consistent with the landing-page voice.
09
Connecta
Strategy diagram · the canvas

The Constellation

Six aqua satellite nodes orbiting a honey brand node — literally the Super Canvas as a logo. Most "AI strategist" of the bunch. Risk: reads as Web3 / DAO if not careful.

— scales down poorly. Best at hero sizes.
10
Connecta
Editorial mark · the voice

The Quote

A bold C with honey serif tails that turn it into stylized quotation marks. Reads as "creator's voice" / "we publish you." Most journalism-coded — pairs beautifully with EB Garamond.

— easy to animate, easy to print, easy to ink-stamp.
Pick a direction — and design from there. These are stylistic drafts in the brand palette. The shapes, proportions, and details are starting points — your final logo should refine and depart from them.
