Pure marks

10 silent logos, one color each.

No wordmark, no second color, no decoration. Each mark is rendered twice — once ink on bone, once bone on ink — so you can see it works in both directions. Pick a direction and refine it yourself.

01
Knot

The Infinity Knot

One continuous loop that crosses itself in the middle. No start, no end. Strategic, intentional, woven.

— what's tied together stays.
02
Connection

The Bridge

Two filled circles, an arc between them. The mark for everything Connecta does — joining one to another.

— the simplest version of the whole product.
03
Spark

The Four-Point Star

A long-armed sparkle — the kind that floats around a viral post. Tall vertical axis, narrow horizontal. Recognizable at any size.

— for the moment something pops.
04
Direction

The Compass Star

Eight-point star: long primary axes + short diagonal counters. Reads as a compass rose, a north star, a strategy mark.

— here's where you're going.
05
Vision

The Eye

Almond outline + solid pupil. Sees what's about to happen. The Jarvis-as-oracle move, distilled to two shapes.

— it watches the feeds so you don't have to.
06
Hand-drawn

The Lasso

A single line that spirals in twice — like a lasso loop drawn quickly with a pen. Carries the page's hand-drawn personality.

— catch it before it gets away.
07
Creator

The Aperture

Six petals spiraling inward toward a tiny center hole. The camera iris, the bloom opening, the focus point. Geometric and ownable.

— focus is the whole job.
08
Broadcast

The Signal

Three nested arcs over a filled dot — a wifi signal, a sonic bloom, a content ripple. The mark for reach.

— send one, hear thousands.
09
Voice

The Quote

A pair of curly serif quotation marks. Pure typography turned into pure symbol. The mark for "we publish you."

— a creator is just a voice you can hear.
10
Strategy

The Asterism

Three outer dots + one inner dot, joined by thin lines. A constellation. A canvas reduced to its essential shape — the Super Canvas as a glyph.

— a plan, drawn in three points.
Pure marks. One color, no text. Each renders on light and dark with equal weight. Pick a direction — the proportions, line weight, and exact shape are starting points you'll refine.
