MARKETING NOTES

C1 — Split Comparison: Price anchoring via contrast. The brain sees $0 next to $79 and the decision becomes "free vs paid" instead of "buy vs don't buy". Most effective for users comparing plans.

C2 — Countdown + Perks: Urgency (7 days ticking) + concrete value (specific features). The "250 credits to keep" line is a loss-aversion hook — they get something even if they cancel. Best for feature-motivated users.

C3 — Minimal Confidence: For the cautious buyer. Strikethrough pricing, exact billing date, progress bar, and 3 specific trust signals that address the top 3 fears: "will I be charged?", "will I forget?", "is it hard to cancel?". The secondary "I have questions" CTA catches users who aren't ready — better to route them than lose them.
